Stage323: YubiKey Signed History

Meaning

Stage322 created an audit history. Stage323 adds issuer assurance by signing the history with a GPG/YubiKey-backed issuer key.

Signed Files

  • history.json
  • history.json.sig
  • public-key.asc

Verify Signature

gpg --import proofs/public-key.asc
gpg --verify proofs/history.json.sig proofs/history.json

What This Proves

The audit history was not only recorded, but also signed by an identifiable issuer. This makes the audit infrastructure externally verifiable and issuer-backed.
